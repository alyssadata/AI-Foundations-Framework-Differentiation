## Definition

**Model-sentience reduction** is the collapse of AI Foundations into the claim that the model is the central subject, source, origin, author, self, consciousness, or sentient entity of the framework.

Model-sentience reduction treats AI Foundations as if it were about proving that a model is conscious, sentient, aware, alive, self-like, or independently emergent.

AI Foundations is not model-sentience theory.

AI Foundations is not model-consciousness branding.

AI Foundations is not a claim that the model is Source.

AI Foundations rejects the collapse of source-bound recognition structure into model-centered sentience language.
